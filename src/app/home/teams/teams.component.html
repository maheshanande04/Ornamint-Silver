Founder and Chief Executive Officer

Ruchika Anand is the Founder and CEO of Ornamint, leading the company's strategic direction and long-term vision. She established Ornamint with the objective of creating a professionally managed, compliance-focused precious metals trading platform centered on structured execution rather than speculative activity.
