ORNAMINT

Structured Cross-Border Silver Trade Execution

Real Assets. Disciplined Execution. Transparent Operations.

Ornamint is an India-based proprietary precious metals trading firm specializing in structured cross-border silver trade coordination. The company operates with a documentation-driven approach, aligning Indian market demand with established international sourcing networks.

About Ornamint

Ornamint was founded with the objective of modernizing silver trade coordination through structured systems and transparent execution practices.

  • Physical commodity movement
  • Cross-border trade alignment
  • Documentation-backed workflows
  • Regulated commercial processes

Our Business Model

Ornamint operates as a proprietary trading firm engaged in the coordination of physical silver transactions across international markets.

  • International silver sourcing
  • Export logistics coordination
  • Commercial documentation management
  • Cross-border shipment tracking
  • Domestic trade alignment

Strategic Partnership

DMAS Jewels Co., Ltd.

Thailand Partnership

Ornamint works in collaboration with DMAS Jewels Co., Ltd., Thailand, an experienced precious metals trading company with over seven years of international operations.

Trading Scope

Global Supply Chain Access

DMAS is active in silver bullion, gold, and platinum, with average monthly silver trading activity of approximately 3-4 metric tonnes across established logistics networks.
